### Description (4000 characters max)

Gym Form Coach turns your phone into a personal form coach. Just prop up your phone, start your set, and get real-time feedback on every rep.

**How it works:**
Point your camera at yourself while you exercise. The app uses on-device AI to detect your body position and analyze your form in real time. After each rep, you get an audio cue and haptic feedback telling you what to fix — or confirming your form is solid.

**4 exercises supported:**
- Squat: Tracks knee path, depth, and torso angle
- Deadlift: Monitors back rounding, hip-shoulder sync, and bar path
- Push-up: Checks hip alignment, elbow angle, and range of motion
- Overhead Press: Watches for back arch, lockout, and symmetry

**Per-rep scoring (0-100):**
Every rep gets a numeric form score based on multiple dimensions. A clean rep scores 95+. A rep with a major form break scores 50-70. Track your improvement over time with detailed score trends.

**Multi-set workouts:**
Run through multiple sets with a configurable rest timer (60/90/120/180 seconds). See per-set breakdowns in your session summary.

**Guided workout programs:**
Follow structured programs like Squat Foundations, Full Body Basics, or the Push-up Challenge. Programs use form-based progression — you advance when your form meets the threshold, not just when you show up.

**Track your progress:**
- Personal bests and workout streaks
- Weekly form report with score trends and insights
- Rep-by-rep breakdown with fatigue detection
- Session history with exercise filtering

**Your data stays private:**
All processing happens on your device. No video is recorded. No data is uploaded. No account required. Export your training data as CSV or JSON anytime.

**Perfect for:**
- Self-coached lifters who want to improve form without a trainer
- Beginners learning proper technique
- Intermediate lifters tracking form consistency across sessions

Download Gym Form Coach and start getting real feedback on every rep.

### What's New (for version 1.0.0)
Initial release! 4 exercises, per-rep scoring, multi-set workouts, guided programs, and weekly form reports. All processing happens on your device — no account needed.

### App Review Notes
This app uses the camera solely for real-time pose estimation to analyze exercise form. No video is recorded or uploaded. All ML processing happens on-device using TensorFlow.js. The app makes no network requests in production. For testing, we recommend trying the Squat exercise — prop the phone 6-8 feet away and perform a few squats to see form analysis in action.
